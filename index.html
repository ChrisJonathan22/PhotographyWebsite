Wedding and Portrait photography across the North East of Scotland - from Aberdeen through Aberdeenshire and Moray to Inverness

Photography provides a unique way to capture those important events in our lives - the joy, the love and the memories. Everyone has a camera these days but how many of the millions of images taken will survive to record our lives today. Through the eye and camera lens of the professional photographer like Andrew Gransden that these memories are preserved for your children and grandchildren to see.

Your wedding will be one of the most important days in your lives and we aim to capture it is our discrete and non-intrusive style. If you are marrying in Scotland we can offer you that special coverage from Aberdeen to Inverness from the shores of the Moray Firth to the Mountains across Aberdeenshire, Morayshire and Inverness-shire. Against the stunning backdrop of the rugged Scottish landscape, Andrew Gransden creates compelling images of your marriage. He specialises in Scottish Destination weddings for couple coming or returning to Scotland to get married. He has happy couples in England, Germany, Spain, Canada, Japan and the United States of America (USA) who have done just that. Andrew is also a fluent German speaker (Deutsch gesprochen). With the thousands of photographs you have of your family, how many are missing one important person - you. We also have our own fully equipped studio for that special portrait to hang in your home. You are most welcome to view a small proportion our work on this website and should you like what you see please contact Andrew to discuss your plans and wishes. You will not be disappointed.
